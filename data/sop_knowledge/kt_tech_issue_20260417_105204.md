# Tech Issue with Loss Being Marked - Escalate and Resolve Loss

TECH ISSUE + LOSS BEING MARKED: If a captain reports a scan not working OR a process cannot be initialized AND loss is being marked on them as a result - two-track resolution: (1) Tell captain to go to the Log10 team WITH PROOF (screenshot of the technical error). (2) Escalate the ticket to L2_LOSSES internally. Tell the captain we will also resolve the loss once the tech issue is confirmed - they will not be held liable for system-caused losses. Action = escalate to L2_LOSSES.
