# SOPs — Orders & Planning

## Forward Leg

**Category:** Orders & Planning
**TAT:** 12 hours
**Keywords:** load|volume|rto|cod

### Scenario
L1 agent puts the hub code into the Orders and Planning View query and will reply according to the “L1_remarks” column.

### L1 Process (What agent must do)
L1 agent puts the hub code into the Orders and Planning View query and will reply according to the “L1_remarks” column. The L1_Remarks are as follows:- Improve your RTO Performance by x % and as demand increases in your polygon, you may see an increase in load. In this case, reply with this template . For now, this is the volume that we are offering. As demand increases in your polygon, you may see an increase in load. In this case, reply with this template . Escalate to L2
